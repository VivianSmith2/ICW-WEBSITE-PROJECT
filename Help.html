How do i choose the right perfume for me?

The best to choose the right perfume for you is to experiment with different scents. Try different perfumes on your skin and see what you like best. You may also want to consider your personal style and your favorite scents when choosing a perfume.


How do i apply a perfume?

The best way to apply perfume is to spray it on your pulse points such as your wrists, neck and behind your ears. You can also spray perfume on your clothes or hair.


How long does a perfume last?

The longevity of a perfume depends on a number of factors such as the type of perfume, the concentration of perfume oil and your skin type. Generally speaking, perfumes with a higher concentration of perfume oil will last longer.


How do i store a perfume?

Store your perfume in a cool, dark place. Avoid storing perfume in direct sunlight or in humid conditions.
